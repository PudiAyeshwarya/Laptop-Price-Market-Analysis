This project showcases an interactive Laptop Price & Market Analysis Dashboard built to analyze laptop market trends, pricing patterns, brand performance, and hardware distribution. The dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of 1,275 laptops across 19 companies, helping users explore insights related to pricing, operating systems, RAM configurations, GPU/CPU brands, and storage types.

**ðŸ“Š Key Highlights**

**KPI Overview:**
Displays total companies, total laptops, average price (in Euros), and number of available operating systems for quick market understanding.

**OS Distribution:**
A donut chart visualizes the dominance of Windows 10, followed by other platforms like Linux, Windows 7, and Chrome OS.

**RAM vs Price Analysis:**
A scatter plot reveals how laptop prices increase with higher RAM configurations, enabling users to identify performance-to-price relationships across brands.

**Brand Price Contribution:**
A horizontal bar chart highlights total price contribution by major brands such as Dell, Lenovo, HP, Asus, MSI, and Acer.

**GPU & CPU Insights:**
Dedicated visualizations compare laptop counts by GPU brands (Intel, Nvidia, AMD, ARM) and distribution across CPU models, helping analyze hardware preferences in the market.

**Brand Availability:**
A bar chart shows which brands dominate in terms of product availability.
